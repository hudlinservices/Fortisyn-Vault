# Fortisyn

**The holding company that owns everything.** Fortisyn is the parent umbrella above all 11 corporate entities. Every company in the structure rolls up to Fortisyn, with centralized IT (Hudlin Services) and software development (Python Slayers) providing the backbone for all subsidiaries.

## Group Architecture

The Fortisyn Group operates through high internal synergy — specialized entities support the operational needs of others rather than each entity building redundant infrastructure.

**Centralized backbone:**
- **Hudlin Services** provides IT infrastructure, networking, VLANs, and cloud services to every entity
- **Python Slayers** builds software and automation for all internal entities

**Strategic R&D loop:**
- **La Dolce Niente** property serves as the primary real-world testbed for **Soleria Technology** home automation systems

**Content-to-commerce pipeline:**
- **Literary Imprint** publishes books by **Roy Hudlin**
- **Auron Media** is the creative engine — produces content, video, branding, and social media
- **Mercova Retail** provides white-label storefronts for branded merchandise

**Asset protection:**
- **Aurora Legacy** is the trust company that holds and protects assets across the umbrella
