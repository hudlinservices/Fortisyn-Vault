# Creative Work

Roy's creative identity. The artist behind the consultant.

## Photography

- Captures moments, perspectives, and emotions
- Preferred aesthetic: atmospheric digital painting over hyper-realistic photography
- Color palette: deep forest greens, warm golden-amber, soft sage, terracotta accents — no cold blues or greys
- 16:9 aspect ratios, 1792x1024 or 2560x1440 — technical precision for digital display
- Excludes people, animals, text, and structures from scenes — pure, untouched nature
- Uses specific hex codes: #2d5a27, #d4a843
- Imagery conveys sanctuary, invitation, and stillness
- Photographic poetry: Embers of the Golden Hour, Where Ocean Meets Jungle, Sunset's Promise

## Poetry

- Writes poetry "that hits between the chest and the gut"
- 3 published books: Echoes of Dusk (sunsets), Ocean's Embrace (beaches), Veils of Mist (mountains)
- Writes about life's contrasts — light and shadow, silence and storm, ephemeral and eternal
- Photography + poetry combined — multimedia experience

## Metal Art

- Designs and shapes metal into unique pieces
- Welding as creative expression — "shaping metal into something unique"

## Books

- **The Art of Stillness** — mindfulness, finding peace in a fast-paced world (The Mindful Way Trilogy, Book 1)
- **Echoes of Dusk** — poetry + sunset photography (Costa Rica Poetry Trilogy)
- **Ocean's Embrace** — poetry + beach photography, 40 photographs
- **Veils of Mist** — poetry + mountain photography, 50 photographs
- **Sloth Adventures: Hank Gets Lost** — children's book (first of planned trilogy)

## Brand

- **MindTechArt** — mindfulness + creativity + technology
- **Narrative Alchemy** — storytelling as a timeless art
- Helps people with fast-paced, always-on lives struggling with exhaustion and burnout
- Goal: help people slow down and find tools to bring back peace and relaxation
