# Aurora Legacy

**The trust company.** Holds and manages assets across the entire Fortisyn umbrella. Legal/financial entity for asset ownership and estate planning.

**URL:** [auroralegacy.com](https://auroralegacy.com)

## Role

- Asset holding and protection
- Trust administration
- Estate and legacy planning

## Structure

Aurora Legacy is the **trust** layer at the top of the [[40-Resources/holding-company-trust-structure|holding-company & trust structure]]: Trust → Holding Company ([[10-Projects/fortisyn/_corporate-hub|Fortisyn]]) → operating companies. The name deliberately omits "Hudlin" (privacy by obscurity) and — by design — does not need the word "Trust" in it. See the concept page for how the asset-protection firewall works.
