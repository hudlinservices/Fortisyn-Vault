# Soleria Technology

**Smart home automation.** Products tested at La Dolce Niente in Costa Rica. Built for comfort, security, and convenience — with an eye to creating solutions others can use.

**URL:** [solariatechnology.com](https://solariatechnology.com) *(note: domain uses "a" — sol**a**riatech)*

## Products & Services

- Smart home automation products for residential environments
- Focus areas: comfort, security, and convenience
- Real-world tested at La Dolce Niente before broader release
- Goal: build scalable solutions that others can eventually use

## R&D Loop

The La Dolce Niente property in Costa Rica serves as the **primary testbed and deployment site.** The cycle:

1. Develop automation prototypes
2. Install and test at La Dolce Niente (real-world conditions)
3. Refine based on usage data
4. Productize for other clients

This gives Soleria a unique advantage — every product ships with real-world validation.
