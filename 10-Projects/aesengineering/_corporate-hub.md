# AES Engineering

> **External client — not a Fortisyn entity.** AES Engineering Ltd is the primary client of [[10-Projects/innovatience/_corporate-hub|Innovatience]] and, through that engagement, the main source of revenue funding the broader [[10-Projects/fortisyn/_corporate-hub|Fortisyn Group]].

**URL:** [aesengr.com](https://www.aesengr.com) · **Tagline:** *"Design A Better Tomorrow."*

## Who They Are

AES Engineering Ltd is a Canadian consulting engineering firm specializing in **electrical engineering, lighting design, and technology solutions for the built environment**. Founded in **2001**, the firm has grown to roughly **150 people across four offices** in British Columbia and Alberta. Headquarters: 505 Burrard St, Vancouver, BC.

## Services

Full-spectrum electrical and technology engineering for buildings and infrastructure:

- **Power distribution** & electrical design
- **Lighting design**
- **Communications / low-voltage** systems
- **Audio-visual (AV)** systems
- **Security** systems
- **Control systems** & building automation
- **Life-safety** systems and electrical code expertise
- **Commissioning**, EV charging infrastructure, energy solutions, 3D modeling

Project sectors (thousands of completed projects): office towers, educational institutions, healthcare facilities, industrial buildings, transportation infrastructure, hospitality, research laboratories, recreational arenas, and high-end residential. Experienced in LEED Gold/Platinum and Living Building Challenge projects.

## Engagement with Innovatience

[[10-Projects/innovatience/_corporate-hub|Innovatience]] delivers technical systems consulting to AES Engineering. Known active work streams (per the Fortisyn status report and project stubs):

- **PMO Setup** — project management office tooling (`/home/projects/aesengineering/pmo-setup/` — docs/, flows/, memory/, powerbi/, scripts/)
- Building-systems / facilities project support consistent with AES's AV, security, and control-systems practice — e.g. Building Intercom Systems, Parkade Entrance, Granville Loops, 360-Camera

This single client relationship is the **revenue engine** of Fortisyn: Innovatience bills AES, and that income funds the group's other ventures.
