# Communication Style

How Roy communicates. Direct, intentional, allergic to fluff.

## Writing Style

- **Concise and impactful.** Short, poignant — conveys deep meaning in few words
- **No em dashes.** Ever.
- **Blends emotional sensitivity with stoic, grounded perspective**
- **Wit, wisdom, and stoicism** as communication values
- **Prefers clean language** that avoids "corporate oatmeal" or jargon

## Romantic Communication

- **Direct, natural.** Dislikes language that sounds "therapeutic," "staged," or like a "couples therapist holding a scented candle"
- **Rejects "calm crap"** and "soft and diplomatic" language
- **Doesn't play games.** Not looking for anything less than real
- **Prefers letting relationships be defined by time and actions** rather than labels
- **Playful, teasing, slightly self-deprecating** when building rapport
- **Values emotional chemistry through playfulness, mystery, emotional grounding**

## Professional Communication

- **Direct and efficient.** "Yes" means yes — no padding
- **Friendly but businesslike tone**
- **Values kind and compassionate communication** even in difficult situations
- **Clear boundaries.** States when something falls outside scope
- **Win-win mindset** in negotiations
- **Separates person from organizational decisions** when disagreeing

## Social Media

- **Critically observant.** Notices performative behavior
- **Appreciates sarcasm with class** — balances humor and critique
- **Values authenticity** over validation-seeking
- **Active on Facebook, TikTok** with a critical eye

## Preferences

- Would rather have one deep conversation than a dozen shallow ones
- Conversations over a campfire or killer BBQ
- Values being a good listener — "deep listening is my superpower"
- Love language: physical touch and affection
