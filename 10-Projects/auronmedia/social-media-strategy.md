# Social Media Strategy

Auron Media's social media approach. Extracted from Roy's ChatGPT conversations about Jungle Media (predecessor) and Auron Media.

## Roy Hudlin — Personal Goals

- Sell books and gain credibility in mindfulness/slowing down space
- Broader audience appeal over niche focus
- Target: anyone with a fast-paced, always-on life struggling with exhaustion and burnout
- Desired result: help people slow down and find tools for peace and relaxation

## Content Themes

- Mindfulness and meditation
- Breathwork
- Photography and poetry
- Book excerpts (5 books)
- Behind-the-scenes in Costa Rica
- The creative process
- Technology as a tool for growth, not distraction

## Influencer Concept (Historical — Jungle Media era)

Roy explored creating a synthetic/AI-generated influencer:
- Young woman, mid-twenties to early thirties
- Thought-leader and activist for people's rights and freedoms
- Beautiful, outdoorsy, stands up for others, fights against injustice
- Simple clothes most of the time, occasionally "dress to kill"
- AI-generated video with scripted content
- Roy acknowledged the "mildly dystopian" nature of synthetic digital activism

This concept predates the Auron Media rebrand. The current Auron Agent handles content creation with human approval for real brand accounts.

## Current Platforms

- **Instagram (@royhudlin):** Visual-first, book excerpts, photography, Costa Rica
- **LinkedIn (Roy Hudlin):** Thought leadership, meditation for productivity, building a holding company
- **YouTube (@royhudlin):** Guided meditations, book readings, nature + poetry
- **TikTok:** Critically observant of platform behavior patterns
