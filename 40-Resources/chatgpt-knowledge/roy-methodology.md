# Roy Hudlin — Work Methodology

How Roy works. Extracted from years of ChatGPT conversations about AES Engineering projects, bid management, and technical consulting.

## Prompt Engineering

Roy is an experienced prompt writer with a technical and engineering background. His methodology:

- **Plain text over disguised files.** "Plain text was too mainstream" — prefers direct requests over file-based workarounds
- **Iterate until no further improvements.** Verify work, review and extrapolate missed details, iterate until nothing remains
- **Remove ambiguity, fix weak instructions, add missing context, define output format, reduce failure points, tighten wording.** The goal is the most robust, comprehensive AI response every time
- **No em dashes in professional writing**

## Requirements & Document Analysis

Core discipline from years of SOR (Statement of Requirements) work:

- **Exact citations required.** Section number, page number, clause title, addendum number, RFI number
- **Uncertainty marked.** "Needs Confirmation" when evidence is weak or ambiguous
- **Never invent.** No invented decisions, intent, or acceptance status — conclusions from provided documents only
- **Requirement traceability at all times.** Conflicts between documents flagged immediately
- **Structured workflow:** Build SOR baseline → compare addenda → analyze RFIs → produce requirement summaries
- **Classification taxonomy**: Accepted, Rejected, Partially Accepted, Clarified Only, Superseded, Open/In Progress, At Risk, Needs Confirmation
- **Change types:** Clarification, Scope Increase, Scope Reduction, Substitution, Deletion, Deferral, Responsibility Transfer, Administrative Change, Pricing-Only, Conflict/Contradiction, Unresolved Issue
- **Changed requirements:** Show current live requirement AND full change chain
- **Two interpretations?** State both, identify the more likely with reasoning

## Specification Writing

- **NMS/MasterFormat tone.** Division 27, 28 specifications
- **"Shall" phrasing** for formal contract tone
- **Generic language.** "Basis of design: Ruckus Wireless or approved equivalent" — not manufacturer-specific
- **Three-part spec format:** General, Products, Execution
- **Contractor as defined term** for "Communications System Contractor"
- **No bullet points.** Paragraph-style text. No numbering at start (Word handles it)

## Bid Management

- **Procore expert.** Trade Setup for configuration, Bid Form for pricing, RFIs for questions, Manage Bids for tracking
- **Custom bid forms** in Excel/Word for complex scopes (not Procore's line-item builder)
- **Bulletproof submissions.** Signature/date fields, project name/number on forms, addenda acknowledgment, exclusion fields
- **Complex bid packages:** Access control, video management, nurse call, RTLS
- **Bid security:** 10% certified cheque or bid bond, 100% Performance + Labour & Material Payment bond surety letter

## Security Consulting

- **Discovery questions organized by category:** General objectives, building use, access control, video surveillance, card management
- **Camera quality taxonomy:** Monitor/Detect, Observe, Recognize, Identify
- **Design priorities:** UPS backup, redundant communication paths, strategic placement, seamless integration
- **Key projects:** Cariboo Memorial Hospital, Wrinch Memorial Hospital, 980 Howe Street ACS Upgrade, Vancouver Island hospitals (Campbell River, Comox Valley)
