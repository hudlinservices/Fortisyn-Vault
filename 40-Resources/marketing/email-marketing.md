# Email Marketing

Highest-ROI digital channel — average 3,600% return. Aurora Media's most direct conversion tool across all Fortisyn entities.

## Five Email Types

| Type | Purpose | Fortisyn Use |
|------|---------|-------------|
| **Welcome** | First impression, set expectations | New Mercova merchant onboarding, book purchase follow-up |
| **Lead Nurturing** | Build trust over time | Innovatience consulting prospects, Literary Imprint author pipeline |
| **Newsletter** | Regular value, stay top-of-mind | Roy Hudlin MindTechArt insights, Jungle Meditation wellness tips |
| **Dedicated** | Single offer or launch | Book releases (Literary Imprint), limited edition drops (Mercova) |
| **Re-engagement** | Win back inactive subscribers | Cart abandonment (Mercova), lapsed readers (Literary Imprint) |

## Rules

- **Never buy lists.** Build organically through opt-ins, gated content, and promotions
- **CAN-SPAM compliance.** Accurate sender info, clear subject lines, easy unsubscribe — mandatory
- **Segment by behavior.** New subscribers ≠ repeat buyers. Tailor content to where they are
