# Copywriting

Persuasive writing that motivates action. The core skill behind every Auron Media deliverable — website copy, social captions, ad creative, email campaigns, product descriptions.

## Core Formulas

### PAS — Problem, Agitate, Solve
1. **Problem** — Name the pain point directly
2. **Agitate** — Make it visceral. Why does this problem hurt?
3. **Solve** — Present your solution as the natural resolution

### AIDA — Attention, Interest, Desire, Action
1. **Attention** — Hook them in the first sentence
2. **Interest** — Build curiosity with specifics
3. **Desire** — Paint the outcome they want
4. **Action** — Tell them exactly what to do next

## Principles

- **Benefits before features.** What the customer gets (outcome) > what the product does (specs)
- **Emotional connection over hard sell.** Modern copy guides interest through storytelling, not pressure
- **Swipe files.** Maintain a collection of high-performing ads, headlines, and landing pages for inspiration
