# SEO Fundamentals

Search Engine Optimization — getting pages to rank in organic search. Every Fortisyn website depends on it.

## How Search Works

1. **Crawl** — Search bots scan pages and follow links
2. **Index** — Pages are stored in a searchable archive
3. **Rank** — Pages are ordered by relevance + authority

If a page isn't indexed, it doesn't exist to search engines.

## The Two Ranking Factors

| Factor | What It Means | How to Build It |
|--------|--------------|-----------------|
| **Relevance** | Does this page answer the query? | Keywords in titles, headings, body, meta tags |
| **Authority** | Is this site trusted by others? | Quality backlinks from reputable sites |

## Core Strategy: Long-Tail Keywords

Target specific multi-word queries, not generic terms:

| Generic (bad) | Long-Tail (good) |
|--------------|------------------|
| "publishing" | "how to self-publish a poetry book" |
| "meditation" | "guided meditation for creative professionals" |
| "ecommerce" | "white-label merchandise platform for authors" |

Long-tail searchers are further down the funnel — they know what they want and are closer to converting.

## Content Volume

Every new page is a lottery ticket. More high-quality, unique pages = more chances to rank. Each blog post, product page, and landing page adds to the total.
