# Growth Hacking

Rapid, creative, low-cost growth tactics. For startups and lean teams — which is every Fortisyn entity.

## Core Philosophy

**Test fast, scale what works.** Run many small experiments. Kill what doesn't move the needle. Double down on what does. This is the opposite of traditional marketing — speed over polish, data over opinion.

## Key Techniques

| Technique | Example | Fortisyn Use |
|-----------|---------|-------------|
| **User Generated Content** | Apple #ShotoniPhone | #MindTechArt reader photos, Jungle Wear customer style posts |
| **Strategic Partnerships** | Co-marketing with non-competitors sharing your audience | Literary Imprint + indie bookstores, Jungle Meditation + yoga studios |
| **Data-Driven Virality** | Spotify Wrapped (+20% downloads in 2020) | Year-in-review for readers, personalized meditation stats |
| **Referral Loops** | "People You May Know" algorithms | Author referrals, reader-to-reader book recommendations |
